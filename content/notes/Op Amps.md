## Enable Math Operators on signals in the analog domain
### 2 Rules:
- Voltage diff between inputs is 0 
- No current flowing through either input 
### 5 Types:
- Inverting Amplifier:
	- $V_b = \frac{-Rf}{R_{in}}V_{in}$
- Summer:
	- $V_0 = \frac{-Rf}{R_1}V_1 + \frac{-Rf}{R_2}V_2 + {...}$
